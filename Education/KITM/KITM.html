Kurukshetra Institute of Technology and Management

Kurukshetra Institute of Technology & Management is a co-educational and private technological institute in Kurukshetra, Haryana. The Institute was established in 1997, and is governed by Shree ram Educational Society. The Institute is affiliated to The Kurukshetra University and is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for its courses.The inspiration to provide all-round development to its students is retrieved from their aim that is 'To reach the forefront of technical and management education by creating highly targeted professional leadership by meaningful transformation of mind and spirit.'

B.Tech

It is a four-year full-time undergraduate program offered in four streams - ECE, CSE, EE, ME

M.Tech

It is a four-year full-time undergraduate program offered in four streams - ECE, CSE

BBA

BBA is three-year duration full-time course affiliated to Kurukshetra University.

MBA

MBA is two-year duration full-time course affiliated to Kurukshetra University.
