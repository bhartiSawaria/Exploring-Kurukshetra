Madhuban Restaurant

Madhuban Restaurant is a property of Hotel Krishna Mahal. This place is recommended for anyone visiting Brahma Sarovar, Birla Mandir, Kurukshetra Panorama and Science Centre, and SriKrishna Museum as it’s located within walking distance from these popular tourist sites. It serves a wide variety of Indian and Chinese dishes. The restaurant's decor is a perfect combination of simplicity and authenticity.If you are looking for a not-so-expensive fun evening, then this place is highly recommended as it's the only restaurant in the city where they have live music performances happening every evening. As per the staff, they prepare food fresh, after the order is placed and guests’ contentment is their primary aim. They also have guest feedback system as they strive for continuous improvement in their services. Please note that it's a pure vegetarian restaurant, and no alcohol is served. It can accommodate maximum 72 guests at one time.
