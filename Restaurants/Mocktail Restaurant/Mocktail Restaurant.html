Mocktail Restaurant

Mocktail restaurant is located inside Saffron hotel, about 1 km away from National Highway 1 and Kurukshetraâ€™s New Bus Stand. It's a fine dining multi-cuisine restaurant where non-vegetarian food is served but no alcohol. Expect a range of Indian, Chinese, Oriental, Mexican and Italian dishes along with delectable seafood. Choose from a range of beverages available here to combine it with the dish you choose to eat. The restaurant is moderately expensive, and they have been serving all kinds of guests. The decor is classy with contemporary furnishings and beautiful artwork on walls. With a capacity of 74 guests at one time, this is a perfect place for business meets and informal gatherings with friends. The management at Mocktail Restaurant believes that no matter how good is one's service, there is always a scope for improvement. Hence, they have a digital customer feedback system known as Text to Business.
