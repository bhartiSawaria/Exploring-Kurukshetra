Divine City Center

The mall consists of four levels. It includes about 135 retail shops. It has a huge parking lot which accommodates around 1500 vehicles. One can find many things at the mall. There are a wide range of Gifts and Stationery, Electronics and Mobile, Eye wear, Food & Beverages, Footwear, Home Furnishings, Jewellery and Accessories, Lifestyle Stores, Luggage, Movies & Entertainment, Personnel Care & Beauty. One of the famous Multiplex, Cinemax is located in the mall. It screens latest Bollywood and Hollywood movies. It has six screens. Many movie lovers and youngsters often visit the mall to watch movies. The food court houses famous food brands like Spaghetti Kitchen, Hinglish, Haldiram’s etc. One can find vegetarian as well as non vegetarian cuisines here.

It consists of first hypermarket of the brand Spar, owned by the Landmark Group. The hypermarket sells grocery, fruit and vegetables, bakery, dairy, take away foods, meat, poultry and fish, wine, beer and spirits, home textiles, personal care, crockery, utensils and kitchen appliances, electronics, IT accessories and much more. One can find brands like Zara, Bebe, Mango, Nine West, Aldo, Lifestyle, Tommy Hilfiger, CK, Veromoda, Body Shop, MAC, Louis Phillip, Van Heusen, Swarovski, Titan, Mother Care, Hush Puppies, Spar, etc. are available here. The mall provides different options for outdoor and indoor advertising. There is a big LED screen on the Mall façade which displays many Ads supported with digital sound. Large hoardings are put on the exteriors of the mall to display latest movies and brands advertisements.
