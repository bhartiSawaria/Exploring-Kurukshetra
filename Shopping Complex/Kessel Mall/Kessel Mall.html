Kessel Mall

Kurukshetra is growing in terms of industrial development and urbanization with expansion of the city. As such, it is witnessing change in lifestyle of its people. The young generation of this city is getting inclined towards contemporary trends that include change in the way they shop as well. Shopping culture of Kurukshetra is getting reinvented with setting up of shopping malls in the city. Tapping on this growing demand of modern shopping experience in city, Kessel Mall is established here by Golden Sparrows Developers Pvt Ltd. Kessel Mall is the first and in fact, the only mall with multiplex for Kurukshetra and its nearby towns. Hence, in a short time since its inception, it has gained success in the form of huge footfall from all parts of Kurukshetra as well as its adjoining areas.

This mall has emerged as architectural milestone for this city and given a boost to its commercial real estate scene. It is aptly situated in the heart of Kurukshetra and it has offered the first of its kind organized retail facility to it.

Kessel Mall in Kurukshetra is spread over large area of 1, 00, 000 sq ft with three floors. The vibrant ambiance of this shopping mall is accentuated by the presence of famous retail stores, food joints, and Glitz Cinemas. The ground and first floor is full of leading brands while second floor has food court and multiplex. Size of shops in Kessel Mall varies from 300 sq ft to 4000 sq ft. Being the only three screens multiplex in Kurukshetra, this mall attracts large number of visitors here.
