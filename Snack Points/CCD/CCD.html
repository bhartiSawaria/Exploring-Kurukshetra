Cafe Coffee Day

About

A local branch of the esteemed Indian café chain, Cafe Coffee Day in Inside Kessel Mall,Ground Floor, Near Ambedkar Chowk, is counted among the best coffee shops of this city.Frequented by coffee lovers from the neighbourhood and the surrounding places, it enjoys valuable patronage of its customers. This renowned café chain is owned and operated by Coffee Day Global Limited.

Special

Cafe Coffee Day in Kurukshetra exhibits a quirky and quaint interior decoration. There are plush sofas and seats that ensure comfort of the guests while they enjoy their share of hot coffee. The staff keeps an ample amount of knowledge about the recipes and helps the customers to select according to their tastes and preferences. The friendly atmosphere and cool vibe of the cafeteria easily attract people from the vicinity. The coffee shop remains functional from 11:00 - 23:00. Residents can also enjoy the recipes by sitting at home as this café has a home delivery option. The green coloured 'Menu' tab on the top of this page enables one to have a quick glance at the menu of this coffee shop. Frequently Ordered Food Items: Some of the most frequently ordered food items from the expansive menu are Hazelnut Cappuccino, Cafe Frappe, Mango Peach Smoothie, Classic Lemonade and Grilled Chicken Wrap. Cafe Coffee Day in Inside Kessel Mall,Ground Floor, Near Ambedkar Chowk, Kurukshetra.
