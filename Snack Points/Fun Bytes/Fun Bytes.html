Fun Bytes

About

Funbytes is a registered trademark under Wadson Foods which is a privately owned company located in Chandigarh, India. Here, at company headquarters people work to here best for franchise and customers. FunBytes began its journey in the month of June 2008 to brand sandwiches and to deliver healthy options to its customer at affordable prices. FunBytes has also engineered its operations repeatedly in its 2 years in to address the special requirements of a vegetarian menu. Vegetable products are 100% vegetarian, i.e, they are prepared separately, using dedicated equipment and utensils. Only pure vegetarian oil is used as a cooking medium. Cheese and sauces are completely vegetarian and egg less.
