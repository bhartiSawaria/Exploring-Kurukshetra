Veggi Green

About

Kurukshetra, also known by the name Dharmakshetra, is widely popular for being the land where the famous war, as depicted in Mahabharata, was fought. Besides being popular for the same, it is home to some amazing restaurants. Non-vegetarian food and alcohol are banned in most of the restaurants since it is considered to be a sacred city. However, the spicy, crunchy and fresh vegetarian food served in Veggi Green never fails to satiate the hunger and taste buds of visitors.

Special

One such snack point is Veggi Green located in DD Colony, Kurukshetra. This serves as famous point in the city of Kurukshetra as it serves only pure vegetarian food. This place is really popular amongst the youngsters and acts as a popular gathering spot, especially for birthday parties. It serves a variety of vegetarian food including deserts, snacks and shakes. It not only provides availaibity of payment option as cash but also provides online payment modes.
