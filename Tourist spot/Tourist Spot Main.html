Sheikh Chilli Tomb

This beautiful tomb and the madarasa are associated with the Sufi saint Abdu'r-Rahim alias Aabd-ul-Razak, popularly known as Shaikh Chehli believed to be the spiritual teacher of the Mughal Prince Dara Shikoh.

Zoo

The zoo is located in Pipli, which lies on the GT Road, very close to Kurukshetra. The zoo houses a range of animals, birds and reptiles. Spread across 27 acres, it was set-up in 1982.

Panorama

Kurukshetra Panorama and Science Centre is a unique Centre that combines science with religion. The main attraction of the Centre is a life-like panorama of the epic battle of Kurukshetra that showcases Mahabharata war.

Kalpana Chawla Planetarium

The Kalpana Chawla Memorial Planetarium is named after the brave daughter of Haryana Dr. Kalpana Chawla. It is located near Jyotisar on Pehowa Road.

Brahma Sarovar

Brahma Sarovar, as its name suggests, is a water tank in Thanesar in Haryana. It is considered sacred by Hindus and is incredibly beautiful. It is said that the water is used by people to take a bath and attain salvation.

Jyotisar

Situated about 12 km from Kurukshetra on Kurukshetra-Pehowa road, Jyotisar is an important place of religious tourism. The holy land of Jyotisar is believed to be the cradle of Hindu civilisation and culture.
